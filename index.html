Shushan Arakelyan

PhD Student

University of Southern California

I’m a fifth year PhD candidate at the University of Southern California. I am part of Intelligence and Knowledge Discovery Lab(INK lab), supervised by Prof. Xiang Ren.

My research interests lie in machine learning for code. I am interested in applications of machine learning fot tasks such as program representation or program analysis, with focus on applications for binary executables and source code.
